Save the Date: May 9, 2018

The Andover family is uniting to make a difference for today’s students—and we want you to stand with Big Blue on PA Giving Day 2018! Your gift—of any size—will count with the thousands of alumni, parents, grandparents, faculty, and friends who are investing in the Andover experience on this special day. Together, we have the power to change lives. Our goal is to create four need-based scholarships and so much more for our talented young people. Your support matters! Your gift will give our students and our school the very brightest of futures.

Ways to Show Your Support

Make a Gift

Big or small, every gift counts. Demonstrate to your friends and classmates that you lead by example and make a gift yourself. Be among the first to support PA Giving Day.

Become an Advocate

Create a GiveCampus account and spread the good word on May 9! Your account will allow you to track your impact.

Create a Matching Gift

Encourage your network to join you—matches of all sizes will make a difference! You can match gifts dollar-for-dollar up to a fixed amount.

Upload a Personal Plea

Use your own words to explain why you give to Andover and encourage your friends to do the same! Upload your video on GiveCampus and share on the advocates tab.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will my gift count towards my reunion?

Yes, any gift to any desigation on May 9th will count towards reunion classyear totals.

Can I make a gift by check?

If you are unable to make a gift online on May 9th, the Annual Giving team can work with you to ensure everyone is able to participate in PA Giving Day.

Do all gifts go to Financial Aid?

While a large portion of gifts from last PA Giving Day supported financial aid, gifts to any designation will count towards our goal of 3,607 donors!

What if I've already made a gift this year?

We are asking the entire PA community to participate in PA Giving Day! Your second gift is greatly appreciated.

Want to get started? Stay tuned for upcoming PA Giving Day news. We'll soon be sharing various resources to help make the day a success!
